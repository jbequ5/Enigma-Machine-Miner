### Core Purpose
This lane transforms accumulated fragments, enriched drafts, and gap intelligence into complete, production-ready toolkits (and related products like curricula or services). Emphasis is on depth in the most commercially and technically valuable gaps.

### End-to-End Product Creation Workflow

**Step 1: Local Draft Accumulation (EM Level)**  
During normal runs or targeted Product KAS hunts, high-signal fragments, high-confidence DOUBLE_CLICK results, and KAS outputs accumulate in the local Product Draft Vault (`products/drafts/[gap_id]/`). Miners can trigger additional enrichment on promising gaps. Only high-confidence experimental data (Double-Click Confidence Score ≥ 0.75) is heavily weighted.

**Step 2: Draft Qualification**  
Local system evaluates against the strict Draft Rubric (detailed below). Only drafts meeting minimum standards are eligible for global submission.

**Step 3: Global Synapse Submission & Ranking**  
Qualified drafts are pushed to the central Economic Subsystem and ranked by Opportunity Ranker for polishing priority.

**Step 4: Synapse Polishing Loop**  
- Enrichment with latest Strategy layer fragments and targeted KAS hunts.  
- Full Synthesis Arbos Pass (multi-proposal generation using diverse reasoning styles, structured debate against global fragments and verifier criteria, iterative refinement with targeted fragment injection, and strict contract enforcement).  
- Benchmark & validation (7D verifier self-check + executability tests).  
- Family variant generation for strong items.

**Step 5: Marketplace Readiness & Listing**  
Final Polished Score evaluation. Approved toolkits are packaged with distilled models/LoRAs, verifier contracts, notebooks, provenance report, benchmark results, and pricing tiers, then listed in the Sage Marketplace.

**Step 6: Post-Listing Improvement**  
7-Day Health Check triggers additional polishing if needed. Real buyer performance data retroactively updates Impact Scores and feeds Meta-RL.

### Draft Rubric (Minimum for Global Submission)
- ≥12 high-signal fragments (average EFS ≥0.72)  
- ≥60% freshness (based on timestamp and relevance)  
- Complete 7D verifier suite + benchmark card  
- ROI hypothesis + BD Relevance Score ≥0.65  
- Full immutable provenance chain

### What Is a Toolkit?
A gap-specific, production-ready package designed for immediate sponsor use and measurable impact.  

**Standard Toolkit Structure**:
- Quantized/distilled Enigma models or LoRAs optimized for the gap
- Full 7D verifier contracts + automated test suites
- Interactive notebooks with worked examples and integration guides
- Implementation roadmap with phased milestones
- Performance benchmark card (EFS, verifier pass rate, real-world metrics)
- Provenance report showing contributor credits
- Pricing tiers (one-time, subscription, performance-based)

**Concrete Example** — Quantum Stabilizer Code Optimization Toolkit  
- Models for syndrome decoding and logical error correction  
- Verifier harness showing 22% average logical error rate reduction  
- Notebooks for different hardware backends  
- Family variant: Lightweight version for smaller QPUs
