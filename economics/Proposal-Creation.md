### End-to-End Proposal Creation Workflow

**Step 1: Opportunity Detection**  
BD Hunter continuously scans EM runs, Scientist Mode DOUBLE_CLICK results, Synapse Chat interventions, and external sources. High Gap Pain Score (≥ 0.78) combined with strong BD Relevance Score flags candidates for proposal development.

**Step 2: Raw Proposal Generation (Local EM Level)**  
Miner or automated agent creates an initial proposal artifact during or after a run. Includes problem statement, preliminary SAGE approach, mock data, and basic verifier plan. Pushed to Product Draft Vault with full provenance.

**Step 3: Global Synapse Enrichment**  
Draft is ranked by Opportunity Ranker. Top candidates receive full polishing:
- Strategy layer supplies ranked, high-centrality fragments.
- Defense layer adds adversarial hardening and robustness rules.
- Intelligence layer injects latest meta-weights and Value NN predictions.

**Step 4: Synthesis Arbos Pass**  
The intelligence core:
- Multi-proposal generation using diverse reasoning styles.
- Structured debate against global fragments, verifier criteria, and commercial fit.
- Iterative refinement (2–4 cycles) with targeted fragment injection.
- Final contract enforcement (7D verifier self-check + executability test).

**Step 5: Readiness Gate & Final Polish**  
Runs full Proposal Readiness Score evaluation. Executes benchmark suite and produces executive summary, implementation roadmap, projected ROI, pricing tiers, and risk analysis. If score ≥ 0.75 and all gates pass → marked “Sponsor-Ready”.

**Step 6: Delivery & Closing**  
Synapse intelligently matches and pitches to sponsors or lists in the Marketplace. Upon landing, revenue is automatically distributed and new high-value challenges are generated for the subnet.

### Key Scoring Formula: Proposal Readiness Score
\[
\text{Proposal Readiness Score} = 
0.35 \times \text{BD Relevance Match} + 
0.25 \times \text{SAGE Technical Strength} + 
0.20 \times \text{Sponsor Fit} + 
0.15 \times \text{Verifiability} + 
0.05 \times \text{Novelty}
\]
**Threshold**: ≥ 0.75 for sponsor outreach or marketplace listing.
