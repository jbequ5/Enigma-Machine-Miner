It turns high-pain gaps identified by Enigma Machine runs into sponsor-ready proposals that close deals and expand the subnet. In simulations, well-polished proposals have a 3–5× higher landing rate than raw drafts. ### Core Purpose
Lane 2 transforms technical insights and gap data into structured, high-impact business proposals that sponsors can immediately act on. It bridges the technical strength of SAGE with real commercial needs.

### End-to-End Proposal Creation Workflow

**Step 1: Opportunity Detection (BD Hunter + Gap Signals)**
- Continuous monitoring of EM runs, Scientist Mode DOUBLE_CLICK results, chat/copilot interventions, and external KAS hunts.
- High Gap Pain Score (≥ 0.78) + strong BD Relevance Score automatically flag candidates.
- BD Hunter scrapes industry reports, sponsor roadmaps, and public pain points to enrich context.

**Step 2: Raw Proposal Generation (Local EM Level)**
- Miner or automated agent generates an initial proposal artifact during or after an EM run.
- Includes: problem statement, initial SAGE approach, mock data, preliminary verifier plan.
- Pushed to the Product Draft Vault with full provenance.

**Step 3: Global Synapse Enrichment**
- Draft is evaluated by Opportunity Ranker.
- Top candidates receive full polishing:
  - Strategy layer supplies ranked fragments and cross-domain patterns.
  - Defense layer adds adversarial hardening.
  - Intelligence layer injects latest meta-weights and Value NN predictions.

**Step 4: Synthesis Arbos Pass**
- Multi-proposal generation → structured debate → iterative refinement → contract enforcement.
- Ensures the proposal is technically strong, verifiable, and commercially compelling.
- Generates executive summary, implementation roadmap, projected ROI, and pricing options.

**Step 5: Readiness Gate & Final Polish**
- Runs Proposal Readiness Score evaluation.
- Executes light benchmark and 7D verifier self-check on embedded test suites.
- If score ≥ 0.75 and all gates pass → marked “Sponsor-Ready”.

**Step 6: Delivery & Closing**
- Synapse can auto-pitch to matched sponsors or list in Marketplace.
- Upon landing, revenue is distributed and new high-value challenges are generated for the subnet.

### Key Scoring Formula: Proposal Readiness Score
\[
\text{Proposal Readiness Score} = 
0.35 \times \text{BD Relevance Match} + 
0.25 \times \text{SAGE Technical Strength} + 
0.20 \times \text{Sponsor Fit} + 
0.15 \times \text{Verifiability} + 
0.05 \times \text{Novelty}
\]

**Threshold**: ≥ 0.75 for sponsor outreach or marketplace listing.

### Concrete Worked Example

**Gap**: Persistent syndrome decoding latency in quantum error correction.

**Raw Draft** (Local EM): Basic stabilizer code proposal with preliminary verifier.

**After Full Pipeline**:
- BD Hunter adds sponsor context from quantum hardware companies.
- Synapse Polishing: Injects 11 new high-signal fragments + cross-domain control theory insights.
- Synthesis Arbos: Refines into full package with phased roadmap, 7D test suite, projected 22% error reduction, and pricing tiers.
- Proposal Readiness Score: 0.82 → Sent to target sponsor.

**Outcome**: Lands $85k deal. New challenge created for the subnet. Contributors (including gap originator) receive proportional revenue share. Performance data feeds back into Meta-RL for future improvements.
