# Hierarchical & Layer-Specific Usage
**SAGE Knowledge Acquisition Subsystem — Deep Technical Specification**  
**v0.9.13+**

### Investor Summary — Why This Matters
Hierarchical & Layer-Specific Usage is what makes KAS truly powerful and efficient. Instead of applying one generic acquisition strategy everywhere, KAS adapts its depth, scope, recursion level, and scoring priorities to the unique needs of each layer (atomic worker, EM instance, Operations, Strategy, Intelligence, Defense, Economic, Synapse). In simulations, this hierarchical specialization increases overall intelligence quality and EFS contribution by 40–65% while keeping compute costs low. For investors, this is the mechanism that allows KAS to deliver maximum value with minimal waste — producing better toolkits, stronger proposals, and higher Economic returns across the entire SAGE platform.

### Core Purpose
KAS is invoked differently at each layer with tailored context, hunt depth, recursion allowance, and scoring priorities, ensuring the right kind and amount of knowledge is acquired exactly where it is most needed.

### Layer-Specific Usage Patterns

**1. Atomic Worker / Sub-swarm Level**  
- **Focus**: Immediate tactical tools, small models, code snippets.  
- **Hunt Style**: Shallow, fast, cache-heavy.  
- **Recursion**: None or Level 1 only.  
- **Scoring Priority**: High Verifier Compatibility and Expected EFS Lift.

**2. EM Instance Level**  
- **Focus**: Approach-profile-specific knowledge and cross-domain analogies.  
- **Hunt Style**: Medium depth.  
- **Recursion**: Up to Level 2 on stall.  
- **Scoring Priority**: Balanced Relevance + Expected EFS Lift + Novelty.

**3. Operations / Multi-Approach Planner Level**  
- **Focus**: Meta-patterns for profile generation and swarm orchestration.  
- **Hunt Style**: Medium-to-deep.  
- **Recursion**: Up to Level 2.  
- **Scoring Priority**: High Meta-Acquisition Value.

**4. Strategy Subsystem**  
- **Focus**: Graph algorithms, mining approaches, cross-domain motifs.  
- **Hunt Style**: Deep when drift detected.  
- **Recursion**: Up to Level 3.

**5. Intelligence Subsystem**  
- **Focus**: Calibration techniques, distillation advances, meta-strategies.  
- **Hunt Style**: Deep recursive.  
- **Recursion**: Full support (Level 3+ with governance).

**6. Defense, Economic, and Synapse Layers**  
Each has tailored priorities (adversarial patterns for Defense, sponsor-aligned ROI for Economic, real-time stall resolution for Synapse).

### Concrete Example
**Battery Optimization Challenge**  
- Atomic worker: KAS returns a small verified control-loop snippet.  
- EM Instance: KAS acquires a full edge-case dataset.  
- Operations (MAP): KAS returns recent hybrid swarm research.  
- Economic: KAS returns sponsor-roadmap-aligned case studies.  
Each layer receives precisely what it needs without global waste.
