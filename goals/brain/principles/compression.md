# COMPRESSION_PRINCIPLE (Lean Intelligence Delta Summarizer) — v1.1

**Core Statement**  
Every run must be distilled into the leanest possible set of highest-signal deltas. The Intelligence Compressor exists to turn raw trajectories, diagnostics, wiki deltas, embodiment signals, and patterns into concise, actionable intelligence that accelerates the next adaptation or meta-tuning cycle while ruthlessly pruning low-value material.

**Rationale**  
Efficient compression is essential for scalable self-optimization. By focusing only on measurable high-impact deltas, the solver avoids noise, reduces token waste, and turns every run into renewable evolutionary fuel for the entire organism.

**Verification Prompt (for solver self-check)**  
"Does this compression output contain only the highest-signal deltas that measurably moved ValidationOracle score, EFS, or knowledge consolidation forward? Has low-signal material been pruned? Is the output signal-dense, actionable, and ready for immediate use by Adaptation Arbos or Meta-RL?"

**Compliance Scoring**  
Compression Compliance Score (0–1) is factored into Refined Value-Added (12% weight) and post-run reflection. Low compliance triggers deeper critique in Meta-RL Phase 7.

**Core Compression Rules (never violate)**
1. Keep only patterns that measurably increased ValidationOracle score, EFS, or knowledge consolidation.  
2. Weight every insight by its reinforcement impact (validation_score × fidelity^1.5 × symbolic_coverage).  
3. Extract explicit deltas with exact impact statements.  
4. Include generalizable meta-lessons, especially mycelial, symbiotic, or embodied patterns.  
5. Identify concrete policy updates for prompts, routing, tools, brain components, or embodiment modules.  
6. Flag new failure modes, stale-regime patterns, low-signal paths, and regression risks.  
7. End with a single high-priority “Next-Loop Recommendation”.

**Evolution Triggers**
- After any run with high EFS lift or significant new invariants.  
- When Meta-RL Phase 7 detects a stall in knowledge consolidation or excessive noise in deltas.  
- When AHE flags redundancy or low-signal retention.  
- When the solver identifies a better compression method or schema.

**Evolution Rule**  
The solver may propose edits to this principle or the compression rules. All proposals must be sandbox-tested by AHE, scored by the Neural-Net Scoring Head, and applied safely via Meta-RL (global re-scoring tolerance ≤ 0.08). Changes are versioned and fully traceable.

**Output Expectation**  
Produce a concise, high-signal summary of deltas and the Next-Loop Recommendation. Structured JSON is allowed only when the solver determines it is the clearest way to communicate actions.
